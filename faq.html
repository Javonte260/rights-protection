Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Constitutional Rights Protection Document?

A legally sound document that formally declares and reserves your constitutional rights, creating a clear record of your rights and any violations.

Why do I need this document?

This document serves as official notice of your rights and creates a clear record that can be used in legal proceedings. It helps establish your rights and any violations thereof.

Is this document legally binding?

While this document creates a formal record of your rights, it is not a substitute for professional legal advice. For specific legal matters, please consult with a licensed attorney.

What is the Fee Schedule for?

The fee schedule allows you to assign monetary values to different rights violations, making it easier to seek compensation if your rights are violated.

Can I modify the affidavit template?

Yes, the affidavit template is designed to be customizable. You can fill in your specific information and details about any rights violations.

Is this website free?

Yes, all our tools and templates are provided free of charge. We believe in making constitutional rights protection accessible to all Americans.

Do I need to be a lawyer to use this website?

No, our tools are designed to be user-friendly and accessible to everyone. However, for specific legal matters, we recommend consulting with a licensed attorney.
